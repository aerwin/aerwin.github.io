It's been just one month since IBM Bluemix reached general availability, but we already have another round of Bluemix UI updates! The new features include enhancements to collaboration, catalog, solutions pages, and responsiveness. You'll also notice updates to Bluemix Documentation with improved search, filtering, and mobile rendering. And, finally, there was a healthy dose of general bug squashing and usability tweaks. Check out my write-up on the Bluemix Developers Community for the details.
